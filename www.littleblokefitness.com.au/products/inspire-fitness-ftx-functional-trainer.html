    Product Description



    Inspire's FTX for functional training allows you to have complete freedom of movement in any direction or plane while performing weight resistance exercises. These unrestricted exercises are designed to move the way your body naturally moves. Many of the freestanding exercises incorporate core, secondary, and stabilizing muscles, giving you a more efficient workout.

    The Inspire Fitness FTX is an excellent training machine for athletes interested in sport specific exercise movements or for people looking to strengthen their overall bodies, increase stamina and coordination or lose weight.


    Features

    • Frame: Heavy duty rectangular and round tubular
    • Powder Coat Finish: The Electrostatic Powder Coat offers a durable, maintenance free finish
    • User-friendly Adjustments: The quick,one-handed adjustable high/low pulley system adjusts to more than 30 height positions for all of your exercise needs
    • Dual Independent Weight Stacks: The dual weight stacks allow you to work each side of the body independently for a more balanced workout.
    • Split Resistance: .5 resistance ratios provide a smooth, continuous feel and a longer range of motion for more versatility in your exercise movements.
    • Weight Stacks: 2 x 75kg Commercial Weight Stacks with solid guide rods and centre drilled weight plates
    • Included Accessories: Tricep Rope, 2 x Single Handles, and EZ curl bar and a multi-function weight belt.
    • Dimensions: Depth 101cm x Width 137cm x Height 215cm

    The Inspire FTX boasts exceptional engineering, high quality linear and roller bearings, fibreglass reinforced nylon pulleys, high strand low resistance soft coated cables, precision fabrication and premium grade coatings on all metal surfaces which only says one thing…. a super smooth machine with exceptional quality that will last a lifetime.

    Warranty is 10 years in a commercial environment and lifetime for domestic use so you can rest assured this unit will operate at it's optimum you for as many years as you continue to train. 

     

    Optional Accessories

    • FT1 FID bench (FIDB)
    • Leg extension developer attachment
    • Preacher Curl attachment
